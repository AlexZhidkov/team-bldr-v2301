Team Builder App Social Events Organiser

Welcome to a social event organisation app designed to make planning and coordinating events easier for you and your friends! The simple interface makes it easy to create events, invite team members, and keep track of attendees.

With this app, you can also collect money from your team members to pay for events or venues using Stripe integration. No more chasing down payments or dealing with awkward money situations!

The app is developed by Alexander Zhidkov. Feel free to connect on LinkedIn or send me an email.

You can find the source code on GitHub. Please raise an issue if you find any bugs. And check out the Discussions to share your ideas and feedback.

Thank you for using the app and I hope it helps you plan some amazing social events with your friends!
